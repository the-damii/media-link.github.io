About us

We had a humble beginning. With an apt philosophy, small is big. Media Link Ltd. was born. Our birth was the necessary tonic that was to breathe life into outdoor business practice in Nigeria. Prior to 1980, it was obvious that outdoor advertising business needed fresh and dynamic ideas. All these were to come in place in September 1983, when two young and vibrant minds: Messrs Babu Akinbobola and ’Kole Ademulegun branched out from one of the nation’s top advertising agencies, Grant Advertising, to set up Media link Limited. Our mission was clear. To change the face of Outdoor Advertising, by creating non-conventional sites and being proactive to our clients’ needs. Media Link Ltd., a dominant name in the Nigerian outdoor advertising industry, specializes in highly professional outdoor displays. Our innovativeness has given us an edge above our counterparts for we have not only changed the face of outdoor advertising in Nigeria by being pro-active to our client’s needs, at every milestone, we have emerged more knowledgeable, more discerning and more purposeful.

Our mission is to remain the professional, forward looking outdoor agency, ready to satisfy our clients for the maximum success of their advertising objectives, thus contributing to the growth, development and improvement of outdoor advertising in Nigeria.
